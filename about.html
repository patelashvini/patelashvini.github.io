Bio

Welcome! I am Ashvini Patel, an M.A. student in Sanskrit (Department of Sanskrit, University of Delhi), deeply passionate about learning, teaching, and research in this timeless field.

Sanskrit, with its profound literature and rich philosophical heritage, has always inspired me to explore its depths and share its beauty with others. My academic journey revolves around studying ancient texts, understanding their cultural and spiritual significance, and making them accessible to modern audiences.

I aspire to contribute to the preservation and revival of Sanskrit through innovative research and engaging teaching methods. Join me as I explore the wisdom of this classical language and its relevance in today’s world.
